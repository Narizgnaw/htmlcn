Intelligent investment

Both investment decision-making and judgment on the market need abundant professional knowledge, mass news information and long-term market data. It is necessary for investors to not only understand the emotion and psychology of the public, but to overcome their own mood swings as well. At present, the rapidly growing technology of Tianhong Robo-Advisor has advantages in solving these problems, which is able to offer steady capital proposals. Besides, being better and faster than the manual data-handling capacity and speed, it helps improve the accuracy of judgment on the market.

The Department of Intelligent Investment Consulting aims to establish a FOF (fund of funds) investment decision-making framework with market timing as the core. To this end, the company is actively strengthening its current investment style and expertise in asset management with Internet elements and mindset.

A Positive Online Experience

Geared toward the specific needs of customers in the mutual fund market with approaches of strategic combination and FOF, the investment consulting service is modulated and commercialized to create a positive online experience with the e-commerce, big data and other new technologies.
A Coherent and Convenient Pool of Investment Vehicles

In 2015, Tianhong launched the “Rongyibao” series of 19 index funds covering broad-based index, various industries and categories, which have become investment vehicles featuring comprehensive underlying assets and low fees in China. They, coupled with money market funds and bond funds under Tianhong management, constitute a convenient and coherent pool of investment vehicles supporting the intelligent investment consulting service.
A Whole Package to Share with Other Financial Institutions

Tianhong will assist any institution interested in intelligent investment consulting to set up a basic framework featuring specific products and systematic workflow.

About Us

Established on Nov. 08, 2004, Tianhong Asset Management is one of the nationwide publicly offered fund management companies authorized by China Securities Regulatory Commissionwith registered capital of RMB 514.3 million Yuan. In 2013, Tianhong Asset Management promoted the first Internet fund – Yu’ebao, jointly with AliPay. As the fund manager of Yu’ebao, Tianhong Asset Management breaks a new ground for the Internet finance.
