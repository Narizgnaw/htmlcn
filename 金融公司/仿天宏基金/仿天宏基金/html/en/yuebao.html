  1. Yu’ebao

    Yu’ebao is China’s first ever Internet fund specially designed for Alipay.
    It remains the largest money market fund in China, known in full as Tianhong Yu’ebao Money Market Fund.
    Putting security and liquidity first, the company delves into the economic and policy climates, market status, and money supply and demand, and designs and adjusts portfolios based on stringent risk control to bring in impressive returns for investors.


    Launch

    In May 2013, the first Internet fund product was launched in China.
    The first of its kind, Yu’ebao is specially designed for Alipay.

  2. Present Situation

    It is a mutual fund covering the most individual clients in China, with more than 260 million subscribers as at June 30, 2016.


    It is the largest money market fund in China by assets under management whose worth stands at RMB 816.3 billion.

Yu'ebao Sentiment Index

Yu'ebao Sentiment Index is a sentiment index designed with big data analysis model and cloud computing technology to reflect subscribers' investment willingness, based on billions of subscribers and trading data.

On November 5, 2015, THFund adopted the big data analysis method to prepare and publish the "Yu'ebao Investment Willingness Sentiment Index" ("Yu'ebao Sentiment Index") based on the daily transaction behavior and data of over 200 million Yu'ebao subscribers.

The index subdivides transaction behavior based on the business scenarios of Yu'ebao, screens the funds that subscribers directly or indirectly invest in the stock market, and creates a standardized index of data after eliminating the effect of IPO, holidays and income.  

The advantage of Yu'ebao Sentiment Index is that it can be published on a daily basis, a frequency higher than that of bank-securities transfer data. Besides, it includes only the data of participation in the stock market, excluding IPO data. That makes Yu'ebao Sentiment Index more valuable.
